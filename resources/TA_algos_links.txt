Technical Analysis algorithms
1. Average True Range (ATR) over a period of 14 days.
2. Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD) with simple moving average method and 26 days & 12 days for the slow and fast periods respectively.
3. Money Flow Index (MFI) over a period of 14 days.
4. FastK and FastD values of Stochastic Oscillator using 14,3, and 3 days for FastK, FastD, SlowD respectively.
5. Directional Movement Index (DMI) using 14 days
6. Commodity Channel Index (CCI) using 20 days, and 0.015 as the constant to apply to the mean deviation.
7. Relative Strenght Index (RSI) using 14 days and weighted moving average.
8. Price Rate of Change (ROC) over 252 or 126 trading days.
9. The Chaikin Accumulation / Distribution (AD) line.
Source : Comparing Technical and Fundamental indicators in stock price forecasting - Erhan Beyaz, Firat Tekiner, Xiao-jun Zeng, John A. Keane, School of Computer Science, University of Manchester, Manchester, UK
